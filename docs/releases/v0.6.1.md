## General Settings Consistency

- Shows `Label Group` and `Signal Intensity` under General Settings for every
  product family, not only Lens Profiling.
- Keeps `Label Group` visible as a read-only value derived from Uplink ID.
- Locks `Uplink ID` for ready-made profiles; it is editable only when `Custom`
  is selected.
- Includes Signal Intensity in the normal write/export/import field set.
